ABOUT

     Blue Ladder Construction Company, established on December 30, 2020 and held by STC East Africa Investment Limited, is a stone processing and production company situated on 40 acres of land just 25 kilometers from Kampala. Based on a geological exploration report by Muhonerezi Bright Consulting Geologist, the company has identified approximately 5 million tons of rock available for use in construction projects, with a sustainable mining period of approximately 10 years. Since commencing formal rock processing and production in August 2022, the company has maintained a daily production capacity of around 1,200 tons of high-quality stone. At Blue Ladder Construction Company, we are dedicated to shaping the earth's beauty into symbols of human progress, guided by the principles of innovation, sustainability, and excellence.
